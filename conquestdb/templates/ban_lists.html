Ban Lists

Cards are banned using "Cycle." cards. These cards are shown at the bottom of this page.

First Dawn bans the following cards: Bonesinger Choir (Eldar), Squiggoth Brute (Orks), Corrupted Teleportarium (Chaos), Archon's Palace (Dark Eldar) and Land Speeder Vengeance (Space Marines).

Last Dawn bans the following cards: Sowing Chaos (Chaos), Smasha Gun Battery (Orks), The Prince's Might (Chaos) and Purveyor of Hubris (Chaos).

Virgin Dusk bans the following cards: Doom (Eldar), Exterminatus (Space Marines), Mind Shackle Scarab (Necrons), Crypt of Saint Camila (Space Marines) and Warp Storm (Chaos).

It should be noted that First Dawn works differently to the other ban lists. First, it has a limit, meaning that if you play two Squiggoth Brutes which get removed from the game by the card, you can play a third one without it being removed. Second, since the card is played, it can be cancelled by Nullify or similar card effects, which can allow the card to enter play despite the ban. However, in a competitive game it is highly unlikely that either of these limitations will come up during a game.

There is no formal system for announcing card erratas; Team Apoka includes the new version of a card in a pack.

Due to this, it is more difficult to track what the errata cards are. That being said, this should be a complete list of all erratas.
